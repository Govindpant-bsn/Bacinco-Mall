A day at Bacinco

From your first coffee to the last credits rolling.

Bacinco changes pace through the day — follow it down.

07:00 — 11:00

Morning

Doors open early for walkers, runners, and the first coffee of the day. The atrium is quiet, the light is soft, and the cafés are already pulling shots.

  • Mall opens at 10am — café row from 7am
  • Free, covered parking for early visitors
  • Quiet hours before 10am, ideal with a pram
11:00 — 17:00

Afternoon

This is when Bacinco fills up — fashion, electronics, home, and everything in between, spread across three easy floors with wide, bright walkways.

  • 180+ stores across 3 floors
  • Personal shopping desk on Floor 1
  • Stroller and wheelchair loans, free
Browse the store directory
17:00 — 21:00

Evening

The food court warms up and the rooftop restaurants catch the last light. Tables fill from six, so it's worth booking ahead on weekends.

  • 24 restaurants and cafés, every cuisine
  • Rooftop dining with skyline views
  • Kids' menu and play corner included
See where to eat
21:00 — 22:00

Night

Last screenings, last games, last laps of the arcade. The mall winds down slowly, with lights low and music softer, until the doors close at ten.

  • Cinema, bowling, and arcade open till close
  • Live music on the atrium stage, Fri–Sat
  • Security walk every floor till lights-out
